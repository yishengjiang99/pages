Engine Notes

      

        The generator now thinks more like a small fugue machine than a simple invention machine. It builds a subject, tonal answer, countersubject, later restatements, episodic sequential material, and overlapping entries. The piano roll scrolls with a centered playhead. MIDI export writes the symbolic score directly. WAV export renders the same score offline in the browser.
